Our Activities & Impact

Explore the hands-on initiatives transforming Youths & communities

Tree Planting

We organise and also participate in tree planting events. We plant trees with our memeber schools and memeber persons across Uganda
These trees are mainly fruit trees because they provide both food and increase the vegetation cover as well thus combating both climate change and food security. See more >>

Community Gardening

Our Community Gardening program empowers young people to transform local spaces into thriving green hubs that promote sustainability, food security, and environmental awareness. Through hands-on activities, participants learn practical skills such as organic farming, composting, and water conservation while working together to cultivate shared gardens. These spaces not only provide fresh, healthy produce but also strengthen community bonds and encourage teamwork.

Climate Education

Through the young people, we teach climate education causes, effects and prevention. In addition, we also take possible actions to prevent it. These actions include treeplanting events, foodwaste-prevention program, and community cleanup campaigns

CleanUp Campaigns

Our Cleanup Campaigns bring young people and community members together to clean streets, schools, markets, and other public places. We collect trash, teach people how to keep the environment clean, and encourage teamwork. These activities help make our communities clean & beautiful hence more safe to leave in!

Eco Youths Network

Our mission is to empower young people to take meaningful action in protecting the environment through education, innovation, and community engagement.
We strive to build a generation of environmentally conscious leaders who promote sustainability and climate resilience.
